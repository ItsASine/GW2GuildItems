GW2GuildItems

A 'spreadsheet' of the items the GW2 guild owns and items related transactions

Goals

  1. Have a list of names of who has donated to the guild, and how many items have been donated by each
  2. Have a list of items currently in all guild locations, stash and treasury
  3. Have a list of all item related transactions in the guild, as a master list
  4. Make it generic so many guilds could use it should they choose
